Dr. Ademola Iyanda Ebeloku

Rector

Dr. Ebeloku was born on 11th July 1967 in Ila Orangun, q23 Osun State, he started his elementary school at St. Michael Primary School, Ila Orangun and after the demise of his grandma, he moved to Ile-Ife to complete his elementary school in 1981. He then proceeded to Ansar-Ur-Deen Grammar School, Ile-Ife from 1981 to 1986 for his high school. This transmission from a Missionary school to Islamic College sharpened his tolerance and stirred him clear of religious segregation and sentiments.

  • B.Sc. in Business Administration: University of Ilorin (1994)
  • Master of Business Administration: University of Ilorin (2002)
  • M.Sc. in Management Science: University of Ilorin (2006)
  • Ph.D. in Business Administration: University of Ilorin (2018)
  • PGDE in Education (PGDE): National Teachers Institute, Kaduna (2007)

Dr. Ademola Iyanda Ebeloku
Rector

Dr. Ademola Iyanda Ebeloku is a Nigerian academic and administrator currently serving as the rector of the Federal Polytechnic, Ile-Oluji. He previously served as the acting rector of Lens Polytechnic, Offa, and dean of the School of Business and Management Studies at the Federal Polytechnic, Offa. Dr. Ebeloku holds a PhD in Business Administration with a specialization in Human Capital Management from the University of Ilorin.

Mrs. Rachael Olufunke Ogunbodede
Bursar

Mrs. Rachael Olufunke Ogunbodede was appointed as the Bursar of the Federal Polytechnic, Ile-Oluji (FEDPOLEL), Ondo State, in April 2020 by the Federal Government. Before this, she worked at Rufus Giwa Polytechnic, Owo, from 2001 to 2020, where she rose to the position of Deputy Bursar. At FEDPOLEL, she has been actively involved in committee memberships and extra-departmental engagements.

Dr. Jerome Idiegbeyan-Ose
Librarian

Dr. Jerome Idiegbeyan-Ose, an experienced professional librarian, was appointed as the Polytechnic Librarian of the Federal Polytechnic, Ile-Oluji (FEDPOLEL), Ondo State.
