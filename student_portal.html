STUDENT REGISTRATION PROCEDURE

How do I Login?
Fresh Student
Login with your Application/UTME Reg. Number as Username and your Surname (in CAPITAL LETTER OR UPPERCASE) as Password You will be required to supply your valid Email Address and Phone Number and change your password before you can proceed In case you forgot your password, click on the forgot password link "Get it Here" and supply your Application Number, your Registered Email address and Phone Number and your new password and click on submit to reset your password and send to your registered Phone Number and Email Address
Please note: FRESH STUDENTS: Your MATRIC NUMBER will be GENERATED after the confirmation of your school fee payment.
Returning Student
Login with your Matric Number as Username and your Surname (in CAPITAL LETTER OR UPPERCASE) as Password You will be required to supply your valid Email Address and Phone Number and change your password before you can proceed In case you forgot your password, click on the forgot password link "Get it Here" and supply your Matric Number, your Registered Email address and Phone Number and your new password and click on submit to reset your password and send to your registered Phone Number and Email Address

Student Biodata - Click on Student Biodata (All Student) - It is compulsory that you fill the biodata sections accordingly. Under the Upload section, you will be required to upload the scanned copy of your Passport(not more than 1 month old) - click on "Finalize and Preview Biodata Registration" under the Finalize section - After clicking on Finalize, you will be allowed to generate school fee invoice/pay online OR make payment at the bank
Please Note: You will not be able to generate school invoice/pay online OR make payment at the bank if you fail to complete these sections appropriately.

How do I make payment? Please Read this important information
All forms of payment including school fee are available in your profile and payable ONLINE via CARD PAYMENT OR OTHER PAYMENT CHANNELS Follow the steps below to make your payment ONLINE via CARD PAYMENT OR OTHER PAYMENT CHANNELS
Steps/Procedure
- Click on Payment
- Your payment due will be available as Debit under Student Ledger
- To make any payment, you have to select the payment type, session, level, mode and PAY ONLINE IN ANY OF THE CHANNELS OR generate invoice from which your RRR NUMBER will be made available
- Present the RRR NUMBER on the Invoice at any of the Banks listed on your invoice and make the payment on REMITA PLATFORM ONLY
- You have to confirm your payment under the Confirm Payment Section and Print your Official Receipt
- Please note: FRESH STUDENTS: Your MATRIC NUMBER will be GENERATED after the confirmation of your school fee payment.
YOUR MATRIC NUMBER BECOMES YOUR USERNAME AUTOMATICALLY. Navigate to your Dashboard to view the generated Matric Number. Also note that the Matric Number will be sent to your email address.

- Click on Course Registration
- To be eligible for course registration, you must have paid and confirmed your school fee
- Your courses will be displayed for you select appropriately and submit , print and present to the appropriate
- Registration Office for approval
- After approval, your must print another copies, present them for endorsement and submit at your department.
Any further enquiries please visit CBT/ICT Center of the Institution
