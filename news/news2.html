Commencement of 2024/2025 Admissions and Online Credentials Verification

The Federal Polytechnic, Ile Oluji, Ondo State, Nigeria, invites applications for its National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diploma (HND) programmes for the 2024/2025 academic session.

Available National Diploma (ND) Programmes:

  • Accountancy
  • Agricultural Technology
  • Architectural Technology
  • Business Administration & Management
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Cooperative Economics & Management
  • Electrical/Electronic Engineering
  • Fishery Technology
  • Science Laboratory Technology
  • Statistics

Available Higher National Diploma (HND) Programmes:

  • Accountancy
  • Computer Engineering
  • Computer Science
    • Artificial Intelligence
    • Cybersecurity & Data Protection
    • Networking & Cloud Computing
    • Software & Web Development
  • Electrical/Electronic Engineering
    • Electronic/Telecommunication Option
    • Power/Machine Option
  • Statistics

Eligibility Criteria:

ND: Minimum of five (5) O’ Level Credit Passes, including Mathematics, English Language, and three (3) other relevant subjects, at not more than two (2) sittings.

HND: Lower Credit Grade in ND with one-year IT certificate, or Pass Grade in ND with two-year IT certificate. Applicants with ND in Computer Science, or NID qualifications in Software Engineering, Hardware Engineering, or Networking & System Security are also eligible.
