About Federal Polytechnic Ile-Oluji

Federal Polytechnic Ile-Oluji (FEDPOLEL) is a young and vibrant institution of higher learning located in Ile-Oluji, Ondo State, Nigeria. Established in 2014, the polytechnic is committed to providing quality technical and vocational education to empower students with practical skills and knowledge for self-reliance and national development.

FEDPOLEL offers National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diploma (HND) programs in fields such as engineering, business studies, applied sciences, and environmental technology, aligning education with modern industry demands. The serene campus and state-of-the-art facilities provide an ideal environment for academic excellence and innovation.

  • National Diploma (ND)

    Offered in fields like engineering, business studies, applied sciences, and environmental technology.

  • Higher National Diploma (HND)

    Advanced programs that build on ND foundations, designed to meet industry needs.
