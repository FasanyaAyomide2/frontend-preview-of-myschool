Rules and Regulations for Portal Use

  • Always use your assigned login credentials (Matric Number and Password) to access the portal.
  • Do not share your login details with anyone to avoid unauthorized access to your profile.
  • Ensure all information entered is accurate and up-to-date to prevent discrepancies in your records.

Updating Biodata

  1. Log in to the student portal.
  2. Navigate to the "Profile" or "Biodata" section.
  3. Update your personal information (e.g., contact details, next of kin, or address). Ensure that all fields are filled correctly and save your changes.

Making Payments

  1. Log in to the portal and go to the "Payments" or "Fees" section.
  2. Select the type of payment you wish to make (e.g., tuition, registration, hostel fees).
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to generate a payment invoice, and proceed to pay through the provided payment gateway (e.g., Remita).
  4. After payment, ensure you print your receipt for record-keeping.

The Management,

Student Affairs,

As a student of this institution, you are expected to uphold the dignity, well-being, and respect of all members within our community.
